Common questions

The Transcribe button isn't showing on a video

Refresh the page after installing the extension. The button appears on the main video on a page; on feeds it shows when you hover a thumbnail.

The first transcription is slow

The first time you transcribe, the Klipscribe app downloads the speech model once. After that it's cached on your computer and runs much faster.

A video couldn't be grabbed

Some protected videos can't be downloaded directly. Paste the video link into the box, or use Live-capture: play the video and click Stop when done.

Is my audio uploaded anywhere?

No. Everything is transcribed on your own computer. Your audio and transcripts never leave your device.

How do I cancel Pro?

Cancel anytime from the receipt / customer portal link in your purchase email. Pro stays active until the end of the current billing period.

Still need help?

Email us at support@klipscribe.app and we'll get back to you. (Please include your browser and what you were transcribing.)

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Klipscribe ("we", "the extension") is built privacy-first. This page explains what we do - and mostly don't - collect.

Audio and transcripts stay on your device

Transcription runs locally on your own computer (a small local app does the work). The audio we capture from a video and the transcripts we generate are processed on your device and are not uploaded to us or any third party. Your transcript history is stored only in your browser's local storage on your device.

What we don't collect

We do not collect your audio, your transcripts, your browsing history, or personal content from the pages you visit. We do not sell data.

Subscription & payments

Payments and subscriptions are handled by a third-party payments provider (the merchant of record). When you subscribe, your payment details are processed by that provider under their own privacy policy - we never see or store your card details. To unlock Pro features, the extension validates your license key with the provider's licensing service; only the license key is sent for that check.

Local storage

The extension stores your settings, your transcript history, and (if you subscribe) your license key in your browser's local/synced storage so the extension works across sessions and your devices. You can clear this anytime from the extension or your browser.

Third-party sites

Klipscribe runs on the websites you choose to use it on. Those sites have their own privacy policies, which we don't control.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us on the support page.

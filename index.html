Questions

Is my audio uploaded anywhere?

No. Klipscribe transcribes right on your own computer (the app does the work locally). Your audio and transcripts never leave your device.

Which sites does it support?

It works on any site with a video - YouTube, TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, ads, online lectures, and more. For some protected videos you can paste the link or use live-capture.

Do I need to set anything up?

Just two things, both one-click: download the Klipscribe app (it installs itself and runs quietly in the background - no Python, no terminal) and add the browser extension. The first transcription downloads the speech model once, then it's ready.

How do I cancel?

You can cancel your Pro subscription anytime from your receipt/customer portal - no questions asked.
